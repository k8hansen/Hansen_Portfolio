ABOUT

I am an aspiring front-end web developer, interested in uncovering web-based and digital solutions. I feel strongly in free, clear, and open communications, and that digital design and successful web development can provide the most direct route to a relationship between user and product.

My first career as a public art project manager has provided me with a background of delivering organized and timely results, and working with projects diverse in scale, concept, and setting. I am a strong collaborator and comfortable with a no-glory-given, behind-the scenes producer role. I’m most interested in pursuing team-based opportunities and projects.

Committed to ongoing education, I have supplemented my BA/MA degrees with a full-stack bootcamp at Academy Pittsburgh, and most recently a front-end focused bootcamp with Moms Can: Code. I hope to work with future teammates who understand the value of knowledge-sharing and continued learning.

I am a non-media artist focused on new-media expression. Using time-based constructs, I use found digital objects and footage to create new narratives.
